Welcome to Training & Placement Cell

Mr. Manish Raut

Training & Placement Officer

Welcome to our Training and Placement Cell. Cummins College has a dedicated and focused Training & Placement (T&P) Cell that works relentlessly towards counselling, employability training, and providing excellent on-campus opportunities for students. Over the past 11 years, Cummins has consistently achieved remarkable placement results, especially in core engineering domains. This success is the outcome of careful planning, strategic execution, and the untiring efforts of the T&P Cell.

It is the dream of every engineering student to be placed in a reputed company with a rewarding profile and competitive compensation. At Cummins, this dream has turned into reality for many, with several students securing opportunities in dream companies offering packages above 6 LPA, along with excellent engineering roles. The consistent rise in average and highest CTCs year after year remains a testimony to the hard work and determination of both our students and the T&P team.

The training provided by our cell is backed by highly experienced trainers who come with rich industry exposure. Their guidance plays a vital role in shaping the career goals of students and preparing them to face the dynamic demands of the corporate world. We take pride in our strong industry partnerships, with leading companies such as Dassault SystÃ¨mes, Cummins India Limited, Persistent Systems Limited, Rubicon, Zensar RPG Foundation, HCL, KPIT and many more extending their continuous support in enhancing the employability skills of our students.

At Cummins College, the T&P Cell is not just about placements, but about holistic development. Our programs emphasize technical skills, communication skills, aptitude building, and personality development, ensuring that students are industry-ready and confident to take on challenges. We remain committed to creating new opportunities, strengthening our recruiter base, and supporting our students in achieving their aspirations.

Together, we aim to build a future where every student of Cummins College is well-prepared, confident, and successful in their professional journey.
